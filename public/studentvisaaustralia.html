Australia Student Visa from India

A student visa is one of the most important documents you will need when you plan to study abroad. Though it is not very difficult to obtain an Australian student visa from India, you need to be thoroughly familiar with the application process, interview, types of student visas, cost, and everything else so that you apply without making any mistakes. You will be required to apply for the Subclass 500 visa, which can be obtained only based on the guidelines with the SSVF (Simplified Student Visa Framework). Do you know that Indian students pursuing a course in tertiary institutions in Australia can now apply to work without visa sponsorship for up to eight years? Though applying for an Australian student visa is a lengthy process, you can easily get through the entire process easily with the help of expert guidance and detailed information. You can read further to learn in detail about student visa processing, visa application cost, processing time, and everything else you need to know.

Types of Australia Student Visa for Indian Students

The Subclass 500 visa is the most popular type of Australian student visa. However, there are other visa types as well, like Subclass 590 and Subclass 407. They are explained in detail below.
